My Projects


First Project: TDEE, BMR, and BMI Calculator


Description: A calculator that takes your age, gender, height, and level of activity throughout the week, it even asks for your preference of measuring units, Metric or imperial, to calculate your Body Mass Index, Basal Metabolic Rate, and Total Daily Energy Expenditure.
The program would also tell you how much calories you should lose per week and give a calculated guess on how much weight you will lose if you follow the advice of the program.
This project was done for my Object Oriented Programming course it is my first big project so it doesn't have a GUI, all inputs and outputs were done through the IDEs terminal.
